<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave Ultrasound Equipment</li><li>Pulsed Wave Ultrasound Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic Diagnostic Equipment is divided into two main types: Continuous Wave Ultrasound Equipment and Pulsed Wave Ultrasound Equipment. Continuous wave ultrasound equipment emits a continuous stream of ultrasound waves, which are used for Doppler imaging and continuous monitoring of blood flow. On the other hand, Pulsed wave ultrasound equipment emits short pulses of ultrasound waves, allowing for more detailed imaging of tissues and organs. Both types of equipment are essential in the field of diagnostic medicine for accurate and precise diagnosis of various medical conditions.</p></p>
