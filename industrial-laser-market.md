<p><p>The industrial laser market can be categorized into different types based on the technology used. CO2 lasers are gas lasers that are commonly used for cutting, engraving, and marking applications. Fiber lasers use optical fibers as the gain medium to generate laser beams and are known for their high efficiency and reliability. Solid-state lasers use a solid medium such as crystal or glass to produce laser beams. Other types of industrial lasers include diode lasers, excimer lasers, and semiconductor lasers, each with their own unique applications and characteristics.</p></p>
