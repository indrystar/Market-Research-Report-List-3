<p><p>Automotive Active Seat Belt Systems come in two main types: Emergency Locking Retractor (ELR) and Automatic Locking Retractor (ALR). ELR systems automatically lock the seat belt during sudden stops or impacts, providing instant protection to the occupant. ALR systems, on the other hand, require the seat belt to be pulled out fully and then automatically lock in place, ensuring a secure fit. Both types of systems are designed to enhance safety and provide better protection to vehicle occupants in the event of a collision or sudden braking.</p></p>
