Thermoelectric air conditioners use the Peltier effect for cooling, compressor-based air conditioners use refrigerant cycles, vortex coolers use compressed air to create a cooling effect, and air-to-air heat exchangers transfer heat between incoming and outgoing air streams. Each type offers different cooling capabilities to meet the specific needs of electrical cabinets.</p></p>
