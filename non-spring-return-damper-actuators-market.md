<p><p>Non-Spring Return Damper Actuators are categorized into different market types based on their control mechanism. Two-Position Control actuators operate in a simple on/off manner, moving the damper to fully open or fully closed positions. Three-Position Control actuators provide intermediate positioning for better airflow control. Other types of actuators may include modulating control, proportional control, or multi-step control for more precise adjustments. Each type of control mechanism offers different levels of control and precision for airflow management in HVAC systems.</p></p>
