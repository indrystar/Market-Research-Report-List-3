<p><p>Sperm Separation Systems are available in various types including On-chip, Electrophoresis, and Dielectrophoresis. On-chip systems involve separating sperm cells on a miniaturized chip, while Electrophoresis systems use an electric field to separate sperm based on their charge. Dielectrophoresis systems separate sperm based on their dielectric properties when exposed to a non-uniform electric field. These different types of systems offer unique advantages and can be utilized in various applications within the field of assisted reproductive technologies.</p></p>
