<p><p>The Spiral Dental Implant market can be classified into three main types based on their design: Cone, Conical, and Cylindrical implants. Cone implants have a tapered shape that mimics the natural tooth root, providing stability and support. Conical implants have a wider base that gradually narrows towards the top, allowing for greater surface area contact with the bone. Cylindrical implants have a straight design that is ideal for narrow or limited spaces. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of stability and integration with the surrounding bone structure.</p></p>
