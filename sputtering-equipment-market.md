<p><p>Sputtering equipment is categorized into different types based on the method used for the process. Physics sputtering involves the physical bombardment of a target material to release particles onto a substrate. Electronic sputtering involves the use of high-energy electrons to eject atoms from a target material. Etching sputtering is used for precision etching applications. Chemical sputtering involves the use of chemical reactions to sputter material onto a substrate. Each type of sputtering equipment serves specific purposes in various industries.</p></p>
