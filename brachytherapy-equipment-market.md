<p><p>Brachytherapy equipment market is segmented into high dose rate (HDR) and low dose rate (LDR) options. HDR brachytherapy involves delivering a high dose of radiation over a short period of time using a remote afterloader device, making it suitable for treating various cancers. On the other hand, LDR brachytherapy involves continuously delivering a lower dose of radiation over a longer period, making it suitable for specific types of cancers. Both options have their own set of advantages and are used based on individual patient needs.</p></p>
