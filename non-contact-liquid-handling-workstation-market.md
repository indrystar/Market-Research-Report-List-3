<p><p>Non-contact liquid handling workstations are advanced systems used in various industries for precise liquid transfer without physically touching the substances. These workstations use different technologies such as Acoustic Technology, Immediate Drop on Demand Technology, Positive Pressure Technology, and Droplet Verification Technology. Acoustic Technology uses sound waves to transfer liquids, Immediate Drop on Demand Technology releases precise droplets at high speeds, Positive Pressure Technology applies pressure to dispense liquids, and Droplet Verification Technology ensures accurate droplet volume and placement. Each technology caters to specific applications in the liquid handling market.</p></p>
