<p><p>Disposable electrosurgery electrodes are available in two main types: hand control and foot control. Hand control electrodes are designed to be held by the surgeon during the procedure, allowing for precise and direct manipulation of the electrode tip. Foot control electrodes, on the other hand, are operated by the surgeon using a foot pedal, providing a hands-free option for controlling the energy delivery. Both types of electrodes offer surgeons versatility and control during electrosurgical procedures.</p></p>
