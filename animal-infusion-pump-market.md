<p><p>The animal infusion pump market includes three main types: large volume infusion pumps, syringe infusion pumps, and other specialized infusion pumps. Large volume infusion pumps are used for continuous and high-volume fluid delivery in animals. Syringe infusion pumps are more precise and are commonly used for delivering smaller and more controlled doses. Other specialized infusion pumps cater to specific needs such as drug-specific delivery or specific species requirements. These different types cater to the varied needs of veterinary practices and research facilities in providing precise and effective fluid delivery to animals.</p></p>
