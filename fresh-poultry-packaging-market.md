<p><p>Fresh poultry packaging market offers various types of packaging solutions to extend the shelf life of the products and maintain freshness. Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP) involves changing the atmosphere inside the package to slow down spoilage. Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) tightly wraps the product in a plastic film to protect it and enhance presentation. Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP) uses a combination of vacuum and heat to create a sealed package. Other types of packaging may include active packaging solutions or specialty coatings for added protection.</p></p>
