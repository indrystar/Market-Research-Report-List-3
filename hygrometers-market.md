<p><strong>The Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Resistive</li><li>Thermal</li><li>Gravimetric</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hygrometers are devices used to measure the moisture content in the air. Different types of hygrometers include Capacitive, Resistive, Thermal, Gravimetric, and Others. Capacitive hygrometers measure humidity based on the capacitance change caused by water vapor absorption. Resistive hygrometers measure humidity by changes in electrical resistance. Thermal hygrometers measure humidity by detecting changes in thermal conductivity. Gravimetric hygrometers measure humidity by weighing a sample before and after absorbing water vapor. Other types may include spectroscopic hygrometers or dew point hygrometers.</p></p>
