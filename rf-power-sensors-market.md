<p><strong>The RF Power Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat-based</li><li>Diode Detector-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF Power Sensors are classified into two main types: Heat-based sensors and Diode Detector-based sensors. Heat-based sensors measure RF power by converting it into heat, while Diode Detector-based sensors use diodes to rectify the RF signal and measure the power level. Heat-based sensors are more traditional and commonly used in high-power applications due to their accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, Diode Detector-based sensors are more compact and cost-effective, making them ideal for a wide range of RF power measurement applications.</p></p>
