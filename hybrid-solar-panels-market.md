<p><p>Hybrid solar panels come in two main types - polycrystalline and monocrystalline. Polycrystalline hybrid solar panels are made from multiple silicon crystals, offering a cost-effective solution with slightly lower efficiency. On the other hand, monocrystalline hybrid solar panels are made from a single silicon crystal, providing higher efficiency but at a higher cost. Both types of panels combine solar photovoltaic cells with additional technologies such as thermal collectors, offering a more efficient way to generate electricity and heat water simultaneously.</p></p>
