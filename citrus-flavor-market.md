<p><p>Citrus flavor market can be categorized into four main types: Type I, powder, juice, and oil. Type I citrus flavors are the natural extracts derived directly from citrus fruits. Powdered citrus flavors are usually dehydrated versions of the fruit, commonly used in baking and food manufacturing. Citrus juice flavors are made from extracting the liquid from citrus fruits, providing a fresh and tangy taste. Citrus oil flavors are essential oils extracted from the peel of citrus fruits, often used in aromatherapy and flavoring products.</p></p>
