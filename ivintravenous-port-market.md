<p><ul><li>Fixed Base Type</li><li>Mobile Base Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IVintravenous port market includes two main types: fixed base and mobile base. Fixed base ports are typically implanted under the skin and connected to a catheter, providing a permanent access point for fluids and medications. Mobile base ports, on the other hand, are designed to be easily movable and can be detached from the body for added convenience. Both types of ports offer a reliable way to administer treatments to patients in need of consistent intravenous access.</p></p>
