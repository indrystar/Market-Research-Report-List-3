<p><p>Biodegradable plastic bags are available in various types, including Polylactic Acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), and Starch Blends. PLA is derived from renewable resources like corn starch or sugarcane and is compostable. PHA is produced by bacteria and is both biodegradable and compostable. Starch blends are a mixture of starch and other biodegradable polymers, offering a more cost-effective option. These biodegradable plastic bags provide a sustainable alternative to traditional plastic bags and help reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
