<p><strong>The Flotation Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell-to-Cell Flotation Cell</li><li>Free-Flow Flotation Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cell-to-Cell Flotation Cell involves the transfer of the floated material from one cell to another for further processing. This type of flotation cell is commonly used in rougher and scavenger stages of mineral processing. On the other hand, Free-Flow Flotation Cell allows for a self-aerating flotation cell that does not require an external blower. This type of flotation cell is typically used in cleaner stages of mineral processing where high-quality concentrate is desired.</p></p>
