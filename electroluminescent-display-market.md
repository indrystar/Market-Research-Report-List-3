<p><ul><li>Transparent Thin Film Electroluminescent Display</li><li>Non-Transparent Thin Film Electroluminescent Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electroluminescent displays are categorized into two main types: transparent thin film displays and non-transparent thin film displays. Transparent thin film displays allow light to pass through the display, making them suitable for applications where visibility through the display is important. Non-transparent thin film displays do not allow light to pass through, providing higher contrast and more vibrant colors. Both types of displays are commonly used in electronic devices such as smartphones, TVs, and wearable technology.</p></p>
