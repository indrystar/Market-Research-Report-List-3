<p><p>Blood group typing technology includes PCR-Based and Microarray Techniques, Assay-Based Techniques, and Massively Parallel Sequencing. PCR-Based and Microarray Techniques involve amplifying and detecting specific DNA sequences in blood samples. Assay-Based Techniques identify antigens on red blood cells through immune reactions. Massively Parallel Sequencing analyzes multiple DNA sequences simultaneously, offering high throughput and accuracy. These technologies play a crucial role in blood transfusions, organ transplants, and determining paternity. The market for blood group typing technology is rapidly advancing with the development of new and improved techniques.</p></p>
