<p><p>Battery Grade Graphite market consists of three main types - Crystalline Flake Graphite, Amorphous Graphite, and Vein Graphite (Lump). Crystalline Flake Graphite is characterized by its flake-like structure and high purity levels, making it ideal for lithium-ion batteries. Amorphous Graphite, on the other hand, lacks a defined crystal structure but has good conductivity properties. Vein Graphite, also known as Lump Graphite, is found in veins and is known for its high purity levels and excellent thermal conductivity, making it suitable for battery applications.</p></p>
