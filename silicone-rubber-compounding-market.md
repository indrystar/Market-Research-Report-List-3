FMVQ, or fluorosilicone, is known for its superior resistance to fuels, oils, and solvents, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. The "Others" category includes various types of silicone rubber compounds tailored for specific industrial needs, such as heat resistance, electrical insulation, or abrasion resistance.</p></p>
