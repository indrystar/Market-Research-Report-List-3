<p><strong>The Neuromuscular Blocking Drug Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-polarizing Agents</li><li>Depolarizing Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neuromuscular blocking drugs are used to induce muscle relaxation during surgeries or medical procedures. Non-polarizing agents work by blocking acetylcholine receptors at the neuromuscular junction, leading to muscle paralysis. Depolarizing agents, on the other hand, initially stimulate muscle contractions by mimicking acetylcholine but then prevent further muscle contractions by desensitizing the receptors. Both types of drugs play a crucial role in ensuring smooth and controlled muscle relaxation during medical interventions.</p></p>
