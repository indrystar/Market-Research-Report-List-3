<p><p>Clay coated recycled boxboard comes in two main market types: single clay coated and double clay coated. Single clay coated boxboard is coated with a layer of clay on one side, making it suitable for applications where only one side needs to be printed or displayed. Double clay coated boxboard, on the other hand, is coated with a layer of clay on both sides, making it ideal for applications where both sides will be visible or printed on. Both types offer sustainability benefits as they are made from recycled materials.</p></p>
