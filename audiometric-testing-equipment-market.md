<p><p>Audiometric Testing Equipment Market consists of two main types: Screening Audiometry and Diagnostic Audiometry. Screening Audiometry involves testing individuals with no known hearing issues to identify potential hearing problems early on. This type of market typically caters to schools, workplaces, and public health clinics. On the other hand, Diagnostic Audiometry is used to assess and diagnose hearing disorders in individuals who are already experiencing hearing difficulties. This type of market serves hospitals, audiology clinics, and ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat) specialists.</p></p>
