<p><ul><li>Continuous Transfer System</li><li>Synchronous Transfer System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi automatic assembly machines can be categorized into different types based on their transfer systems. Continuous transfer systems involve a continuous movement of parts along the assembly line. Synchronous transfer systems use indexing motions to move parts from one station to another at timed intervals. Other types of transfer systems include systems that combine elements of both continuous and synchronous transfer. Each type offers specific advantages and is chosen based on the requirements of the assembly process.</p></p>
