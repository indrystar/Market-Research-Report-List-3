<p><p>Oral X-ray films come in three different market types: Green, Half Speed Blue, and Full Speed Blue. Green film is the slowest type, providing the highest image quality but requiring a longer exposure time. Half Speed Blue film is faster than Green, but still provides good image quality. Full Speed Blue is the fastest option, offering quick exposure times but slightly lower image quality. Dentists can choose the type of film based on their specific needs for speed and image clarity during oral X-ray procedures.</p></p>
