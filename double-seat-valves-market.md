<p><p>Double seat valves are classified into different types based on their functions and applications. Shut-off valves are used to completely stop the flow of liquids or gases. Tank bottom valves are specifically designed for use at the bottom of tanks for drainage or sampling. Sealing valves are used to create a tight seal to prevent leakage. Other market types of double seat valves include control valves, pressure relief valves, and divert valves, each serving a specific purpose in various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and chemical processing.</p></p>
