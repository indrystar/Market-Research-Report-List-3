<p><p>3D printing materials market can be categorized into four main types: engineering plastics, photosensitive resin, metallic material, and ceramic material. Engineering plastics offer high strength and durability, making them ideal for industrial applications. Photosensitive resin is commonly used in SLA and DLP printers for accurate and detailed prints. Metallic materials are used for creating strong and functional parts, while ceramic materials are employed for their thermal and electrical insulation properties, making them suitable for specialized applications. Each material type caters to different needs in the 3D printing industry.</p></p>
