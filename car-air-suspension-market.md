<p><p>Car air suspension systems come in various types, including convoluted, sleeve, and others. Convoluted air suspension features a rubber bellow with a ridged design, providing a smooth and comfortable ride. Sleeve air suspension uses a cylindrical sleeve to contain the air, offering better durability and stability. Other types of air suspension systems may include rolling lobe or bellows designs, each with its own set of benefits and drawbacks. These different types cater to varying preferences and requirements of car owners.</p></p>
