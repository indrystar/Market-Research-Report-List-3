<p><p>Ambulatory Electrocardiography (ECG) Market includes Resting ECG Devices, Stress ECG Devices, and Holter Monitors. Resting ECG Devices are used to record heart activity while the patient is at rest. Stress ECG Devices are used to monitor the heart's response to physical exertion. Holter Monitors are portable devices that continuously record the heart's activity for an extended period, typically 24 to 48 hours. These devices are essential for diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions outside of a clinical setting.</p></p>
