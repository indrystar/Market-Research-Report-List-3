IV Fluid Bags are used to administer fluids and medications intravenously. CAPD Bags are used in continuous ambulatory peritoneal dialysis. Blood Collection Bags are used to collect and store blood for transfusions. Urine Collection Bags collect urine for testing. Enteral Feeding Bags deliver nutrition directly into the stomach or intestines.</p></p>
