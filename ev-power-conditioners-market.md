Two key types of EV Power Conditioners are V2H (Vehicle-to-Home) and V2G (Vehicle-to-Grid) Power Conditioners. V2H Power Conditioners enable EVs to power homes, while V2G Power Conditioners allow EVs to feed excess energy back into the grid. These technologies are crucial for maximizing the efficiency and sustainability of EVs in the evolving energy landscape.</p></p>
