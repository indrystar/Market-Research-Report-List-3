<p><ul><li>Spin Column Method</li><li>Magnetic Bead Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Nucleic Acid Extraction Instrument market includes two main types: Spin Column Method and Magnetic Bead Method. The Spin Column Method involves isolating nucleic acids using a spin column, where the sample is loaded onto the column and purified through centrifugation. On the other hand, the Magnetic Bead Method uses magnetic beads to capture nucleic acids, which are then separated using a magnetic field. Both methods offer efficient and automated solutions for nucleic acid extraction in various research and clinical settings.</p></p>
