<p><p>Single-cell genome sequencing technology encompasses various market types, including Next Generation Sequencing (NGS), Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR), quantitative PCR (qPCR), Microarray, and Multiple Displacement Amplification (MDA). NGS offers high-throughput sequencing capabilities, while PCR and qPCR allow for targeted amplification and quantification of DNA sequences. Microarray technology enables the parallel analysis of multiple genes. MDA amplifies genomic DNA from a single cell to generate enough material for sequencing. These different market types cater to the diverse needs of researchers studying single-cell genomics.</p></p>
