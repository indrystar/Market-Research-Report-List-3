<p><ul><li>Parallel Gripper</li><li>Angular Gripper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric modular grippers can be classified into two main types: parallel grippers and angular grippers. Parallel grippers are designed to grasp objects with parallel jaws moving towards each other in a linear motion. These grippers are suitable for handling a wide range of objects with varying sizes. On the other hand, angular grippers have jaws that move in an angular motion, allowing them to grip objects at different angles. This type of gripper is ideal for applications where objects need to be manipulated in various orientations.</p></p>
