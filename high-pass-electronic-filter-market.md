<p><p>High-pass electronic filters are used to allow high-frequency signals to pass through while attenuating low-frequency signals. There are two main types of high-pass electronic filters: passive and active. </p><p>Passive electronic filters rely on passive components like resistors, capacitors, and inductors to filter out frequencies, while active electronic filters use active components like transistors and op-amps to amplify and manipulate signals. Both types of filters offer different advantages and disadvantages depending on the specific application and requirements of the user.</p></p>
