Homopolymeric PVC paste consists of pure PVC resin, while co-polymeric PVC paste includes additional copolymers to enhance specific properties. Both types of PVC paste are commonly used in various applications such as coatings, adhesives, and sealants due to their versatility and performance.</p></p>
