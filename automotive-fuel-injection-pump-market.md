<p><p>Automotive Fuel Injection Pump market is divided into two main types: Common Rail Fuel Injection Pump and Rotary Fuel Injection Pump. Common Rail Fuel Injection Pump uses a high-pressure fuel rail to supply fuel to injectors, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions. On the other hand, Rotary Fuel Injection Pump features a rotating distributor that controls fuel delivery to each cylinder. Both types of fuel injection pumps play a crucial role in delivering the right amount of fuel to the engine for optimal performance.</p></p>
