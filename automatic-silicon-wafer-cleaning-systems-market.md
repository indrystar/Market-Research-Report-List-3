<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic</li><li>Full-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic silicon wafer cleaning systems can be classified into two main types: semi-automatic and full-automatic. Semi-automatic systems require some manual intervention from operators during the cleaning process, such as loading and unloading the wafers. On the other hand, full-automatic systems are completely automated, requiring minimal to no human involvement once the process has started. Full-automatic systems are preferred in high-volume manufacturing environments due to their efficiency and consistency, while semi-automatic systems are commonly used in lower volume settings where cost is a concern.</p></p>
