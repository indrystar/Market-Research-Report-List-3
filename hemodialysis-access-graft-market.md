<p><p>Hemodialysis Access Graft Market offers three main types of grafts: Biological Grafts, Synthetic Grafts, and Biosynthetic Grafts. Biological Grafts are made from natural tissues such as veins or arteries from donors. Synthetic Grafts are man-made materials like PTFE or Dacron. Biosynthetic Grafts are a combination of natural and synthetic materials. Each type of graft has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different patient needs and preferences in hemodialysis procedures.</p></p>
