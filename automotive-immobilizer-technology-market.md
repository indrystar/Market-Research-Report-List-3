<p><p>Automotive immobilizer technology is a security system installed in vehicles to prevent them from being stolen. This technology works by connecting the vehicle's engine control unit (ECU) to an electronic key fob or transponder chip. The vehicle will not start unless the correct key is present, making it difficult for thieves to hotwire or steal the car.</p><p>The Automotive Immobilizer Technology Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period. Manufacturers are developing advanced systems that use fingerprint recognition or facial recognition to authenticate the driver before starting the vehicle. Other trends include the use of advanced encryption techniques and cloud-based security solutions to protect against hacking and unauthorized access.</p></p>
