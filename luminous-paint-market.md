<p><p>Luminous paint includes three main types: fluorescent paint, phosphorescent paint, and radio-luminescent paint. Fluorescent paint absorbs light and then emits it quickly, making it appear brightly colored in natural lighting. Phosphorescent paint absorbs light and slowly releases it over time, creating a glowing effect in the dark. Radio-luminescent paint contains a radioactive material that emits light continuously without needing exposure to light. Each type of luminous paint has unique characteristics that make them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
