Milling utilizes rotary cutters to remove material from a workpiece, producing complex shapes and surfaces. Grinding is used to achieve precision surface finishes and tight tolerances on materials. Each type caters to specific requirements in manufacturing processes, offering high precision, efficiency, and flexibility in their respective applications.</p></p>
