<p><p>The sequencing platform market can be divided into two main types: table-top and large-scale platforms. Table-top platforms are compact, affordable systems that are suitable for small-scale sequencing projects in research labs or clinical settings. On the other hand, large-scale platforms are high-throughput systems that can process a large volume of samples simultaneously, making them ideal for large-scale genome sequencing projects or industrial applications. These platforms offer different levels of throughput, scalability, and cost, catering to a wide range of sequencing needs.</p></p>
