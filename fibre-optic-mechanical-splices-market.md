<p><p>Fibre optic mechanical splices market types include flexible couplings, swivel couplings, capillary couplings, V-groove couplings, and others. Flexible couplings allow for slight movement and flexibility in the connection. Swivel couplings enable rotation of the fibre optic cables. Capillary couplings use capillary action to achieve precise alignment. V-groove couplings hold the fibers in place using a V-shaped groove. Other types of couplings may include different mechanisms or materials for aligning and connecting fibre optic cables.</p></p>
