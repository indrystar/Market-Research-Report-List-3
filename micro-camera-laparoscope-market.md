<p><ul><li>Single-hole</li><li>Porous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro camera laparoscope market includes various types, such as single-hole and porous laparoscopes. Single-hole laparoscopes have a single entry point for insertion into the body, allowing for a more focused and precise view during surgery. Porous laparoscopes, on the other hand, have multiple entry points which provide a wider field of vision but may require more skill to maneuver effectively. Both types of laparoscopes offer advantages and are used in different surgical procedures based on the specific needs of the patient and the surgeon's preference.</p></p>
