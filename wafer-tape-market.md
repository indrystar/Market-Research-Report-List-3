<p><strong>The Wafer Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tape for Grinding</li><li>Tape for Dicing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer tape for grinding is specifically designed to hold the wafer in place during the grinding process, ensuring precision and accuracy. It has strong adhesive properties to secure the wafer firmly in place without causing any damage. On the other hand, wafer tape for dicing is used for the process of dicing the wafer into individual chips. It provides a secure hold during the dicing process and helps to prevent any contamination or damage to the wafer. Both types of wafer tape play a crucial role in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
