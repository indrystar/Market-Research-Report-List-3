<p><p>Food safety testing kits are categorized into different types based on the technology used for testing. PCR-based kits utilize polymerase chain reaction technology to detect and quantify DNA or RNA related to foodborne pathogens. Immunoassay-based kits rely on antibodies to detect specific antigens in food samples. Other types of kits may include those utilizing rapid detection methods such as biosensors or chromatography. Each type of kit offers unique advantages in terms of accuracy, sensitivity, and speed of testing for ensuring food safety.</p></p>
