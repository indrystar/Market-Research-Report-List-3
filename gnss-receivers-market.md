<p><strong>The GNSS Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Frequency GNSS Receiver</li><li>Multi-GNSS Receiver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-frequency GNSS receivers are devices that can receive signals from a single frequency of satellites. These receivers are typically less expensive and are suitable for applications that do not require high accuracy. </p><p>On the other hand, Multi-GNSS receivers can receive signals from multiple global navigation satellite systems, such as GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou. These receivers provide higher accuracy and reliability compared to single-frequency receivers, making them ideal for applications that require precise positioning, such as surveying and navigation.</p></p>
