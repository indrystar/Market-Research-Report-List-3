<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic Straightening Machine</li><li>Fully-Automatic Straightening Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-automatic straightening machines require manual intervention for loading and unloading parts, while the straightening process itself is automated. Fully-automatic straightening machines, on the other hand, are fully automated and do not require any manual intervention once the parts are loaded onto the machine. Both types of machines are used in industries where precision straightening of metal parts is necessary, such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing industries. The choice between semi-automatic and fully-automatic machines depends on the level of automation and production volume required by the user.</p></p>
