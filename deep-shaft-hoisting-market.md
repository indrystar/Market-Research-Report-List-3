Friction hoists use a set of drums and ropes to lift loads, while drum hoists utilize a single drum for hoisting. Blair Multi-rope hoists have multiple ropes attached to the lifting mechanism, distributing weight evenly. These hoisting systems cater to different mining requirements and depths, providing efficient and safe transportation underground.</p></p>
