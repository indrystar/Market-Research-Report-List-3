<p><ul><li>Double Angle Cut</li><li>Chevron Cut</li><li>Helical Cut</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic granulators are classified based on the type of rotor and bed knife they use for cutting plastic materials. Double angle cut granulators use rotor and bed knives with two cutting edges at an angle. Chevron cut granulators have a V-shaped rotor and bed knife design for efficient cutting. Helical cut granulators feature a spiral rotor and bed knife arrangement for continuous cutting. Each type offers specific advantages in terms of efficiency, cutting quality, and energy consumption to cater to different needs in the plastic recycling industry.</p></p>
