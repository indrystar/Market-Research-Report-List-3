Steam indicators are used for autoclave sterilization, Dry Heat indicators for high-temperature sterilization, EO indicators for medical device sterilization, and Hydrogen Peroxide indicators for low-temperature sterilization. Others include indicators for specific sterilization processes such as ozone or radiation. These indicators help ensure that sterilization parameters are met for effectiveness and safety.</p></p>
