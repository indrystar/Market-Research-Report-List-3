<p><p>Diabetic Retinopathy Market involves various treatment options such as laser surgery, injection of corticosteroids, anti-VEGF drugs, and vitrectomy. Laser surgery helps to seal off leaking blood vessels and prevent abnormal blood vessel growth. Injection of corticosteroids reduces inflammation and swelling in the eye. Anti-VEGF drugs block the growth of abnormal blood vessels. Vitrectomy is a surgical procedure to remove blood from the center of the eye. These treatments aim to manage and improve vision in patients with diabetic retinopathy.</p></p>
