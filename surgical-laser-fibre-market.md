<p><p>Surgical laser fibres are categorized into two main types based on their intended use: reusable laser fibres and disposable laser fibres. Reusable laser fibres are designed for multiple procedures and are sterilized between uses, making them cost-effective in the long run. On the other hand, disposable laser fibres are intended for single use only, eliminating the need for sterilization and reducing the risk of cross-contamination. Both types of laser fibres play a crucial role in surgical procedures involving laser technology.</p></p>
