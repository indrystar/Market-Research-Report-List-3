<p><p>Nuclear imaging systems market is segmented into three main types - Positron Emission Tomography (PET), Single-photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT), and Planar Scintigraphy Systems. PET systems use radioactive tracers to produce detailed images of organs and tissues, while SPECT systems utilize gamma rays to create 3D images. Planar scintigraphy systems provide 2D images of the distribution of radiopharmaceuticals in the body. Each type of system offers unique advantages and applications in diagnostic imaging and treatment planning.</p></p>
