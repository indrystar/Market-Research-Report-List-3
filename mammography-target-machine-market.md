<p><p>Mammography target machine market consists of two main types: Full-Field Digital Mammography (FFDM) and Digital Breast Tomosynthesis (DBT). FFDM uses digital technology to produce high-quality images of the breast, making it easier for radiologists to detect abnormalities. DBT, on the other hand, captures multiple images from different angles to create a 3D reconstruction of the breast tissue, improving the accuracy of breast cancer detection. These advanced technologies are transforming the mammography market by offering more precise and efficient screening options for patients.</p></p>
