<p><p>Covid-19 antibody tests are available in three main types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assay. RDTs provide quick results by detecting antibodies in a blood sample. ELISA tests are more sensitive and can quantify the levels of antibodies present. Neutralization Assays are the most accurate, measuring the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus. Each type of test serves a different purpose in identifying and monitoring immunity to Covid-19.</p></p>
