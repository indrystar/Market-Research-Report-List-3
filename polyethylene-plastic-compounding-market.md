<p><p>Polyethylene plastic compounding market is divided into three main types - High-density Polyethylene (HDPE), Linear Low-density Polyethylene (LLDPE), and Low-density Polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE is known for its durability and strength, making it suitable for products like pipes and containers. LLDPE is flexible and has good impact resistance, commonly used in packaging films and coatings. LDPE, on the other hand, is more flexible and has good chemical resistance, often used in applications such as squeeze bottles and films.</p></p>
