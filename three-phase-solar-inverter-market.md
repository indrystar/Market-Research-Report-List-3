<p><p>Three-phase solar inverters are categorized into power frequency, medium frequency, and high frequency inverters based on their operating frequencies. Power frequency inverters operate at the standard electrical grid frequency of 50-60Hz. Medium frequency inverters operate at frequencies higher than power frequency inverters, typically around 1-20kHz. High frequency inverters operate at even higher frequencies, usually above 20kHz. Each type of inverter has its own advantages and suitable applications depending on the specific requirements of the solar power system.</p></p>
