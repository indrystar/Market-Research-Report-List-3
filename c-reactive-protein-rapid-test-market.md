<p><p>C-Reactive Protein Rapid Test market includes different types such as Immunoturbidimetric Assay, ELISA, Chemiluminescence immunoassay (CLIA), and others. Immunoturbidimetric Assay measures the amount of CRP in a sample by detecting changes in the turbidity of the solution. ELISA utilizes antibodies to detect CRP levels in a sample. Chemiluminescence immunoassay (CLIA) uses light emission to measure CRP levels. Other types of tests may include point-of-care tests or molecular diagnostic tests for CRP detection.</p></p>
