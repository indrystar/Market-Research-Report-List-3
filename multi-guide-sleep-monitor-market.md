<p><p>The multi guide sleep monitor market consists of three main types: EGG monitor, dynamic monitor, and video monitor. EGG monitors measure brain activity during sleep, providing detailed information about sleep patterns and disorders. Dynamic monitors track body movements and heart rate to analyze sleep quality and duration. Video monitors use cameras to record and analyze sleep behaviors and patterns. Each type offers unique insights into sleep health and can help individuals better understand and improve their sleep habits.</p></p>
