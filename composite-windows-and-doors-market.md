<p><p>Composite windows and doors combine different materials to create a durable and energy-efficient product. In the market, two common types are Fiber Reinforced Plastics (FRP) and Wood Plastic Composites (WPC). FRP windows and doors are made of a mixture of reinforced fibers and thermosetting resins, providing strength and longevity. On the other hand, WPC windows and doors consist of wood fibers mixed with thermoplastics, offering a natural look with added durability. Both types cater to consumers looking for high-performance, low-maintenance window and door options.</p></p>
