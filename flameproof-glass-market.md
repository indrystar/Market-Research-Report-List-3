<p><p>Flameproof glass is available in two major types: Single-plate flameproof glass and compound type flameproof glass. Single-plate flameproof glass consists of a single layer of specially treated glass, making it resistant to flames and heat. On the other hand, compound type flameproof glass is made by bonding multiple layers of glass with fire-resistant materials, enhancing its ability to withstand high temperatures. Both types of flameproof glass are used in a variety of applications where fire safety and protection are essential.</p></p>
