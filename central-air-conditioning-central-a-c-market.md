<p><p>Central Air Conditioning (Central A & C) systems come in two main market types: Split System and Packaged Unit. Split System A/C units have components located both inside and outside the home, with the evaporator coil and fan coil inside and the compressor and condenser coil outside. Packaged Unit A/C systems have all components housed in one unit typically located outside the home. Split Systems are more common for residential use, while Packaged Units are often used in commercial settings or homes without space for an indoor unit.</p></p>
