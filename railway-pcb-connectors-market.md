<p><p>The Railway PCB Connectors market comprises two main types: Board-to-board connectors and Wire-to-board connectors. Board-to-board connectors are used to connect printed circuit boards (PCBs) together within electronic devices, allowing for data and power transfer between them. Wire-to-board connectors, on the other hand, are utilized to connect wires to PCBs, enabling external devices to be connected to the circuit board for communication or power supply. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring seamless connectivity in railway systems and other electronic applications.</p></p>
