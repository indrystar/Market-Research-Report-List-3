<p><p>Protein assays market offers various types of assays for protein quantification. Dye-binding assays utilize colorimetric dyes to detect proteins. Other types of assays include fluorescence-based assays, enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA), and chromatography-based assays. Each type of assay offers advantages in terms of sensitivity, speed, and ease of use, catering to different research and diagnostic needs.</p></p>
