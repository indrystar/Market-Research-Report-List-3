<p><p>Plastic Optical Fiber (POF) is classified into two main types: PMMA Type and Perfluorinated Type. PMMA Type POF is made from polymethyl methacrylate, which is cost-effective and widely used in communication systems, automotive applications, and industrial networks. On the other hand, Perfluorinated Type POF is made from perfluorinated polymer materials, which provide superior performance in terms of low attenuation and high bandwidth, making it suitable for high-speed data transmission and medical equipment applications.</p></p>
