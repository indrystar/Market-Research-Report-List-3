<p><p>Lead Acid (SLA) Battery market is divided into two main types: AGM Battery and GEL Battery. AGM Battery, or Absorbent Glass Mat Battery, uses a fiberglass mat to absorb electrolyte solutions, providing higher energy density and minimal maintenance. On the other hand, GEL Battery contains silica gel electrolyte which makes it maintenance-free and suitable for deep cycle applications. Both types offer high energy efficiency, long lifespan, and are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, telecommunications, and renewable energy.</p></p>
