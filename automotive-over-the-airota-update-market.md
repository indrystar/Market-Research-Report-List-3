<p><p>The Automotive Over-The-Air (OTA) Update market includes two main types: Firmware over-the-air (FOTA) and Software over-the-air (SOTA). FOTA involves updating embedded software and code in automotive systems remotely, ensuring optimal performance and security. SOTA, on the other hand, focuses on updating application software and features in vehicles to improve functionality and add new capabilities. Both types of OTA updates are essential in modern vehicles to keep them up-to-date, secure, and competitive in the market.</p></p>
