<p><p>IP-based PBX offers two main market types: Unlimited PBX and Metered PBX. </p><p>Unlimited PBX provides users with unlimited calling minutes for a fixed monthly fee. This option is ideal for businesses with high call volumes or those looking for predictable monthly costs. </p><p>Metered PBX, on the other hand, charges users based on the number of minutes they use each month. This option is suitable for businesses with fluctuating call volumes or those looking to only pay for the minutes they actually use. Both options offer flexibility and scalability for businesses of all sizes.</p></p>
