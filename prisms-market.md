<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Prisms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dispersion Prisms</li><li>Deviation Prisms</li><li>Rotation Prisms</li><li>Displacement Prisms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prisms are used in various industries and applications, each serving a specific purpose. Dispersion prisms separate light into its different spectral components, while deviation prisms deflect light rays at a specific angle. Rotation prisms rotate an image or change its orientation. Displacement prisms shift the position of an image without changing its orientation. Each type of prism caters to different needs in fields such as optics, microscopy, photography, and spectroscopy, offering precision and versatility in light manipulation.</p></p>
