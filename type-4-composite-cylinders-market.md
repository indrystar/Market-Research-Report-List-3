On the other hand, Carbon Fiber Composites are composed of carbon fibers in a polymer matrix, offering superior strength-to-weight ratio and resistance to corrosion. Both markets are growing due to increasing demand for lightweight and high-performance materials in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and marine.</p></p>
