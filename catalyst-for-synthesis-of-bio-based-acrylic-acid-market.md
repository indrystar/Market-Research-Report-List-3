<p><p>The catalysts used for the synthesis of bio-based acrylic acid can be categorized into four main types: Metal Salt Catalyst, Molecular Sieve Catalyst, Microbial Preparation, and Others. Molecular Sieve Catalysts are used for the dehydration of bio-based intermediates to form acrylic acid. Microbial Preparations involve biotechnological processes for the conversion of renewable resources into acrylic acid. Other types of catalysts may include enzyme-based or nanoparticle catalysts.</p></p>
