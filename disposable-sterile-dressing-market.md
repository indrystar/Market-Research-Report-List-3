<p><p>Disposable sterile dressings are an essential part of wound care and come in various types. Hydrophilic colloid dressings are designed to manage moderate to heavy exudate by absorbing moisture while maintaining a moist environment for proper healing. Transparent film dressings are breathable and provide a barrier against bacteria and water while allowing for visual monitoring of the wound. Other types of dressings include foam dressings, alginate dressings, and composite dressings which cater to specific wound types and healing needs.</p></p>
