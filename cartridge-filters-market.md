<p><p>Cartridge filters market includes different types such as Diatomaceous Earth, Activated Carbon, Cellulose, and Perlite filters. Diatomaceous Earth filters utilize fossilized remains of diatoms to trap debris and contaminants. Activated Carbon filters use a highly absorbent form of carbon to remove impurities. Cellulose filters are made from natural fibers and are effective in capturing larger particles. Perlite filters use volcanic rock to filter out impurities. These different types cater to specific needs and preferences of consumers in the filtration industry.</p></p>
