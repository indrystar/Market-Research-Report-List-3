<p><p>Floor-standing infrared heaters come in three main types based on their heating range: far, medium, and near. Far infrared heaters emit long wavelengths that heat objects and surfaces in the room, making them ideal for larger spaces. Medium infrared heaters emit medium wavelengths that heat the air in the room, suitable for average-sized spaces. Near infrared heaters emit short wavelengths that heat people and objects directly, making them perfect for small spaces or targeted heating needs. Each type offers different benefits depending on the space and heating requirements.</p></p>
