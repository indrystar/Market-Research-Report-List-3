<p><strong>The IC LED Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Current</li><li>Regulated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC LED Driver market types can be categorized into Constant Current and Regulated drivers. Constant Current drivers control the current flowing through the LED to ensure a consistent and stable brightness, making them suitable for applications where a consistent light output is required. On the other hand, Regulated drivers maintain a constant voltage output regardless of fluctuations in input voltage, providing protection to the LED from overvoltage. Both types cater to different lighting requirements and play a crucial role in the efficient operation of LED lighting systems.</p></p>
