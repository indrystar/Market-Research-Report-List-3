<p><strong>The Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-borne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Reactive</li><li>Hot-melts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sealants are available in four main types based on their formulation: water-borne, solvent-borne, reactive, and hot-melts. Water-borne sealants are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up, while solvent-borne sealants are known for their fast drying time. Reactive sealants cure by chemical reaction, providing strong adhesive properties. Hot-melts are solid at room temperature but melt when heated, allowing for easy application. Each type of sealant offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
