<p><strong>The Submarine Manifolds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Template Manifold</li><li>Cluster Manifold</li><li>Pipeline End Manifold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submarine manifolds are key components of offshore oil and gas production systems. Template manifolds are fixed structures that connect multiple subsea wells to a central hub. Cluster manifolds are used to connect a smaller number of wells in close proximity. Pipeline end manifolds are located at the end of a pipeline and distribute fluids to various subsea facilities. Each type of manifold serves a specific purpose in efficiently managing production and transportation of oil and gas from subsea wells to surface facilities.</p></p>
