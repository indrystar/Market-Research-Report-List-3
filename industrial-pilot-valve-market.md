<p><p>Industrial pilot valve market is categorized into two main types: low pressure pilot valve and high pressure pilot valve. Low pressure pilot valves are designed to operate in systems with lower pressure levels, typically below 1500 psi, and are commonly used in industries such as HVAC and chemical processing. On the other hand, high pressure pilot valves are engineered to withstand higher pressure levels, usually above 1500 psi, and are utilized in industries like oil and gas, aerospace, and automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
