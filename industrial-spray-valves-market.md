<p><p>Linear spray valves are a type of industrial spray valves that produce a straight stream of fluid when opened. They are commonly used in applications where precision spraying is required. Radial spray valves, on the other hand, produce a fan-shaped spray pattern when opened. They are often used in applications where a wider coverage area is needed. Both types of spray valves are essential for various industries such as automotive, electronics, and food processing for dispensing liquids accurately and efficiently.</p></p>
