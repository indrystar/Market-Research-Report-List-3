<p><p>Leaf-Spine Fabric Switches are typically divided into two main market types: Leaf Switches and Spine Switches. Leaf Switches serve as the access layer switches that connect end devices such as servers and storage to the network. Spine Switches, on the other hand, act as the backbone layer switches that interconnect multiple leaf switches together. Both types of switches are essential components in a Leaf-Spine network architecture, providing high bandwidth and low latency connections for efficient data traffic flow.</p></p>
