<p><p>Tumor ablation market includes various types such as Radiofrequency Ablation (RF), Microwave Ablation, Cryoablation, and Other Technologies. RF ablation uses high-frequency electrical currents to heat and destroy cancer cells. Microwave ablation uses electromagnetic waves to generate heat and kill tumor cells. Cryoablation involves freezing and thawing tissue to eliminate cancer cells. Other technologies may include laser ablation, high-intensity focused ultrasound, and irreversible electroporation. These techniques are minimally invasive and can be effective alternatives to surgery for treating tumors.</p></p>
