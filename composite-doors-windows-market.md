FRP is known for its high strength and light weight, making it a preferred choice for commercial buildings. On the other hand, WPC offers a natural wood-like appearance with the added benefit of being resistant to rotting and insects. Both types of composites provide a long-lasting and low-maintenance option for doors and windows.</p></p>
