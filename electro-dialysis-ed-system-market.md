<p><p>Electro Dialysis (ED) System Market includes two types: Continuous Electro Dialysis and Batch Electro Dialysis. Continuous Electro Dialysis involves the continuous flow of solution through the dialysis cell, allowing for a constant process of separation. On the other hand, Batch Electro Dialysis involves separating the solution into batches for processing in the dialysis cell. Continuous Electro Dialysis is often preferred for large-scale industrial applications, while Batch Electro Dialysis is suitable for smaller, more controlled processes.</p></p>
