Diesel STS cranes are powered by diesel engines, providing high power output but also producing emissions. Electric STS cranes operate on electricity, offering cleaner and quieter operation. Hybrid STS cranes combine both diesel and electric power sources for enhanced efficiency and reduced environmental impact.
