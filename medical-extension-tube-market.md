<p><strong>The Medical Extension Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-way Tube</li><li>2-way Tube</li><li>Multipath Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical extension tubes come in three types: one-way tubes, 2-way tubes, and multipath tubes. One-way tubes allow for fluid flow in one direction only, commonly used for IV administration. 2-way tubes have two separate lumens for simultaneous fluid infusion and aspiration, often used in procedures like airway management. Multipath tubes have multiple lumens for various fluid pathways, enabling more complex procedures such as dialysis or cardiology interventions. Each type of extension tube serves different medical purposes based on the required functionality.</p></p>
