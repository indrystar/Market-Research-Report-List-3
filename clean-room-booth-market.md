<p><p>Clean room booths are available in two main types: movable and unmovable. Movable clean room booths are designed to be portable and can easily be moved from one location to another. They are ideal for locations where flexibility is needed, such as temporary clean room setups. Unmovable clean room booths, on the other hand, are fixed in place and cannot be easily relocated.
