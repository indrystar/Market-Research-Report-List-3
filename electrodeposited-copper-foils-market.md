<p><p>Electrodeposited copper foils are available in various thicknesses to suit different applications. Foils between 20-50 μm are frequently utilized in rigid printed circuit boards for their balance of flexibility and conductivity. Copper foils above 50 μm are mainly used in high current applications where high conductivity is required. The thickness of the copper foil plays a crucial role in determining its performance in specific electronic applications.</p></p>
