<p><p>In the Pet Imaging X-ray Flat Panel Detector market, there are two types of detectors: Indirect Conversion and Direct Conversion. Indirect Conversion detectors convert X-rays into light, which is then converted into an electrical signal. This method is more common and cost-effective. Direct Conversion detectors directly convert X-rays into electrical signals, resulting in higher image quality but at a higher cost. Both types have their advantages and disadvantages, and the choice between them depends on factors such as image quality requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
