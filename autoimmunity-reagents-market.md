<p><p>Autoimmunity reagents market types include BLOT, ELISA, IFA, and CLIA. BLOT (Western Blot) is a technique used to detect specific proteins in a sample. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is a common method for detecting antibodies or antigens in a sample. IFA (Immunofluorescence Assay) is used to detect specific antibodies in a sample using fluorescent dyes. CLIA (Chemiluminescent Immunoassay) is a sensitive method for measuring analytes in a sample using chemiluminescence. These techniques play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring autoimmune diseases.</p></p>
