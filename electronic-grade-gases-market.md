Deposition gases are used to deposit thin layers of materials onto semiconductor wafers. Etching gases are used to selectively remove material from the wafer surface. Doping gases introduce impurities into the semiconductor material to alter its electrical properties. Lithography gases are used in the process of transferring patterns onto the wafer surface. Each type of gas plays a critical role in the production of advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
