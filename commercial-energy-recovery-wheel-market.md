<p><strong>The Commercial Energy Recovery Wheel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensation Rotors</li><li>Hygroscopic Rotors</li><li>Sorption Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Energy Recovery Wheel Market Types include Condensation Rotors, Hygroscopic Rotors, and Sorption Rotors. Condensation rotors use the condensation of water vapor to recover energy, while Hygroscopic rotors absorb and release moisture to achieve energy recovery. Sorption rotors use desiccants to remove moisture from the air stream and release it outside, thus recovering energy. Each type of rotor serves a different purpose in energy recovery systems, providing various options for commercial and industrial applications.</p></p>
