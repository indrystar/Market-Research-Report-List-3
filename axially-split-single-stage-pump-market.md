<p><p>Axially split single stage pumps are classified into two main types based on the volute design: single volute pumps and double volute pumps. Single volute pumps have a single spiral-shaped casing surrounding the impeller, while double volute pumps have two spiral-shaped casings symmetrically arranged. Single volute pumps are commonly used in applications where space is limited, while double volute pumps are preferred for applications requiring higher efficiency and reduced radial thrust. Both types are popular choices in various industries for their reliable performance and flexibility.</p></p>
